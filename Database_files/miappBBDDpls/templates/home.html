Welcome to the Plasmid Integrative Database

The Plasmid Integrative Database is a comprehensive resource designed to facilitate research and understanding of plasmids across various environments and host organisms. Our project aims to integrate detailed information on plasmids, their host bacteria, and associated genes to support scientific discoveries in microbiology, genetics, and biotechnology.
